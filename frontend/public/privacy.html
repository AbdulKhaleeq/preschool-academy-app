Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 26, 2025

Wellington Kids Preschool ("we", "our", "us") operates the Wellington Kids Preschool mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number for authentication via SMS OTP using Firebase Authentication.
  • Authentication Data: Firebase generates a secure token upon successful login, which we use to verify and manage sessions.
  • Minimal User Data: We may store role-based access (e.g., Parent, Teacher, Admin) as assigned by the school administrator.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users via OTP.
  • To manage user roles and access permissions.
  • To improve the App and ensure secure access to school-related features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your phone number and authentication data are securely processed by Firebase (Google LLC).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase Authentication, a service provided by Google LLC. Please review Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for parents, teachers, and administrators of Wellington Kids Preschool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.
